Konidela Pawan Kalyan

About the HERO

As an actor, Kalyan primarily works in Telugu cinema and is known for his unique style and mannerisms. He has a huge fan base[4] and is one of the highest-paid actors of Indian cinema. He has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list multiple times since 2013. Kalyan is the recipient of a Filmfare Award South and a SIIMA Award among other accolades.[8]
Kalyan made his acting debut in the 1996 film Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi, but rose to prominence with the dramas Gokulamlo Seeta (1997) and Suswagatham (1998). He achieved stardom for his performance in Tholi Prema (1998), which won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu that year. Kalyan established himself as a leading actor with successful projects such as Thammudu (1999), Badri (2000), Kushi (2001), Jalsa (2008), Gabbar Singh (2012), Attarintiki Daredi (2013), Gopala Gopala (2015), and Bheemla Nayak (2022).
